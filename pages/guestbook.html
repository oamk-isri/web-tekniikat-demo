Guestbook

This page showcases the use of JavaScript to modify the page contents. It does this by simulating a rather dated concept of a guestbook, where visitors could leave a snippet of text indicating their visit.

Nowadays this idea has transformed to the well known concept of a comments section.

The implementation on this page is purely visual, meaning it doesn't transmit or presist any data anywhere. This means that remarks left aren't retained. Maybe we'll get to use local storage or external databases later for this purpose.
